Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small roastery with a passion for exceptional coffee. What started as a dream between two college friends has grown into a beloved community hub where coffee lovers gather.

We source our beans directly from farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Each batch is roasted in-house to highlight the unique characteristics of the origin.
